alt+1,2,3 - to shift between different files.

ctrl+` - to open command prompt.

ctrl+p - to select a file when they are different no of files.

            it is used as an alternative to alt+1 or 2,3 (if they are many files)

ctrl+w - to close single file.

ctrl+w+k - to close all files.

folder/folder2/index.html - to create folders and files directly.
